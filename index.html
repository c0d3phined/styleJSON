styleJSON is a light script, at around 12KB minified, that can 'style' a JSON data-source by supplying a simple, hierarchical object.

  • You may notice the higher execution time on this demo, it is because the timer was counting while the dateFormat library was being lazy-loaded. When the dateFormat script loaded, and subsequent operations completed, then the completed callback is called.
  • Demonstration of the (theoretical) infinite levels that styleJSON can handle.
